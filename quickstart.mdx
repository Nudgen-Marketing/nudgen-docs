## What this guide helps you do

You'll sign up, upload a small contact list, set your brand tone and at least one signature, create a campaign with the wizard, and send it. No credit card required for the free trial.

## Before you start

- A CSV file with at least one contact (email required, name optional)
- About 5 minutes

<Note>
New accounts get a **trial** with limited contacts and emails—enough to run your first campaign. Exact limits depend on your plan; see [Billing](/billing/overview) or the live [pricing page](https://nudgen.net/pricing) for current numbers.
</Note>

**Prerequisites for sending:** You need at least one active contact, at least one brand config, and at least one email signature. The app will prompt you if any are missing.

## Step 1: Sign up

1. Go to [nudgen.net](https://nudgen.net).
2. Click **Start Free Trial** or **Sign in**.
3. Sign in with Google. You're synced to the app and redirected to the **Dashboard**.

## Step 2: Upload your first contacts

1. In the sidebar, open **Contacts**.
2. Click **Upload CSV** (or add a single contact if you prefer).
3. Upload a CSV with an `email` column (and optionally `name`). Add tags if you want (e.g. `trial`, `inactive`).
4. Confirm. Contacts are encrypted and stored. You'll see masked emails (e.g. `j***@g***.com`) until you reveal them.

<Card title="CSV format" icon="file-csv" href="/contacts/upload">
  Column names, validation rules, and size limits are in the upload guide.
</Card>

## Step 3: Configure brand and signature

1. Go to **Brand** in the sidebar. Create or pick a brand config: choose a preset tone (e.g. Friendly, Professional) and optionally add a custom prompt or example emails. Save.
2. Go to **Settings** → **Signatures**. Create at least one signature; you'll attach it when creating a campaign. Save.

These are required so the campaign wizard can generate on-brand copy and append a signature to emails.

## Step 4: Create your campaign (wizard)

The campaign flow has three steps: **Campaign Setup**, **Content Generation**, and **Finalize**.

1. Go to **Campaigns** and click **New campaign**.
2. **Campaign Setup:** Choose campaign type (one-time or drip). Select audience: all contacts, by tags, or manual selection. Optionally add a CTA link (tracked). Pick brand config and signature, and enter a campaign goal (e.g. "Re-engage users who haven't logged in for 7 days"). Check the reach estimate; fix audience if it's zero.
3. **Content Generation:** Click **Generate**. AI creates subject and body. Edit in the editor if needed, use **Desktop** / **Mobile** preview, and **Send test** to email yourself a copy.
4. **Finalize:** Review the summary. For drip, set stop rules (e.g. stop on click or open) if desired. Choose **Send now** or **Schedule** (date and time), then confirm. Emails go out via our email infrastructure with open and click tracking.

## Step 5: Check the dashboard

After sending, the **Dashboard** shows overview metrics (sent, delivered, opens, clicks, CTR, bounces, complaints) and a campaigns table. Use the campaign detail page for recipient-level activity and time-series.

## What happens next

- Draft campaigns can be edited until you send or schedule. Once sent, content is locked but you can view analytics and stop drip steps.
- If you set auto-stop rules, recipients who meet the rule (e.g. click) won't receive later steps in the sequence.
- For more structure, use the [onboarding checklist](/getting-started/checklist).

## Troubleshooting

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="I don't see my contacts after upload">
    Ensure your CSV has an `email` column and valid formats. See the [upload guide](/contacts/upload) for rules and limits.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="The wizard says I have no active contacts">
    Upload or add at least one contact and ensure they're not suppressed or unsubscribed. Check [Contacts overview](/contacts/overview).
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Can I change the generated email?">
    Yes. After generation, edit the subject and body in the editor before you finalize and send.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="How do I stop a campaign?">
    From the campaign list or detail page, use **Stop campaign**. Already-sent emails cannot be recalled.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
