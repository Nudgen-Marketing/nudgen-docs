## What this guide helps you do

You'll sign up, upload a small contact list, set your brand tone, create a campaign, and send it. No credit card required for the free trial.

## Before you start

- A CSV file with at least one contact (email required, name optional)
- About 5 minutes

<Note>
New accounts get a **7-day trial** with up to 10 contacts and 20 emails. Enough to run your first campaign.
</Note>

## Step 1: Sign up

1. Go to [nudgen.net](https://nudgen.net).
2. Click **Start Free Trial** or **Sign in**.
3. Sign up with Google or email. No credit card needed.

## Step 2: Upload your first contacts

1. In the dashboard, open **Contacts** from the sidebar.
2. Click **Upload CSV**.
3. Upload a CSV with an `email` column (and optionally `name`).
4. Add tags if you want (e.g. `trial`, `inactive`).
5. Confirm. Contacts are encrypted and stored. You'll see masked emails like `j***@g***.com` until you reveal them.

<Card title="CSV format" icon="file-csv" href="/contacts/upload">
  Need details on column names, validation, and size limits? See the full upload guide.
</Card>

## Step 3: Configure brand tone

1. Go to **Brand** (or **Settings** → **Brand**).
2. Pick a preset: **Friendly**, **Professional**, **Playful**, or **Urgent**.
3. Optionally add a custom prompt or example emails.
4. Save. This tone applies to AI-generated content in your campaigns.

<Card title="Brand configuration" icon="palette" href="/brand/overview">
  Learn how presets, custom prompts, and examples affect AI output.
</Card>

## Step 4: Create and preview your campaign

1. Go to **Campaigns** and click **New campaign**.
2. Select your audience (all contacts or by tags).
3. Choose your brand config.
4. Enter a campaign goal (e.g. "Re-engage users who haven't logged in for 7 days").
5. Click **Generate**. AI creates the subject line and email body.
6. Use the **Desktop** and **Mobile** preview toggle to check how it looks.
7. Click **Send test** to email yourself a copy before launching.

## Step 5: Send (or schedule)

1. Choose **Send now** or **Schedule**.
2. If scheduling, pick date and time.
3. Confirm. Emails go out via Postmark with open and click tracking.

## What happens next

- The dashboard shows sent, delivered, opened, and clicked counts.
- If you set auto-stop rules (e.g. stop on click), users who engage will not receive later steps in a drip.
- You can edit draft campaigns anytime. Once sent, content is locked but you can still view analytics.

## Troubleshooting

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="I don't see my contacts after upload">
    Check that your CSV has an `email` column and valid email formats. Names are optional. See the [upload guide](/contacts/upload) for format details.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Can I change the generated email?">
    Yes. After generation, you can edit the subject and body in the editor before sending.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="How do I stop a campaign?">
    From the campaign list or detail page, use **Stop campaign**. Already-sent emails cannot be recalled.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
