## What Nudgen does

Nudgen is a SaaS platform for creating, sending, and analyzing retention and lifecycle email campaigns. You re-engage inactive users with AI-crafted emails that feel human, not robotic—personalized, on-brand campaigns that convert instead of generic newsletters nobody opens.

**How it works:**

1. **Pick the right audience** — Import contacts, use a segment (tags or manual selection), or send to everyone. You see a reach estimate before launch.
2. **Generate, edit, and test** — Set your brand tone and campaign goal. AI creates subject and body; you refine in the editor, preview, and send test emails before going live.
3. **Launch with guardrails** — Send once or run a drip sequence with stop rules (e.g. stop on click or open). Track opens, clicks, bounces, unsubscribes, and recipient outcomes in real time.

We send via Postmark, apply sanitization and deliverability checks, and handle unsubscribes and suppression so your reputation stays healthy.
