## What Nudgen does

Nudgen helps you re-engage inactive users with AI-crafted emails that feel human, not robotic. Instead of generic newsletters that nobody opens, you send personalized, Duolingo-style retention campaigns that convert.

**Three steps:**

1. **Upload** — Import a CSV of contacts. We encrypt every email and name with AES-256 before it reaches our database.
2. **AI generates** — Set your brand tone (friendly, professional, playful, or urgent). GPT-powered AI creates unique email content for each campaign.
3. **Track & auto-stop** — Send via Postmark, track opens and clicks, and stop automatically when users engage. No more over-emailing.
