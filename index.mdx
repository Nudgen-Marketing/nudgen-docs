## What Nudgen does

Nudgen is built for **shop owners and growing SMEs** who need retention email automation to boost customer lifetime value. Instead of spending weeks managing complex setups or hiring an expert, Nudgen helps you launch useful lifecycle campaigns in minutes, personalize them for each contact with AI, and stop follow-up automatically when customers already engage.

The simplest way to think about Nudgen is this: it helps lean teams get useful email automation live fast, with the control you need and none of the usual setup drag.

**Simple 3-step process:**

1. **Choose your audience** — Import contacts, use simple tags (like "Abandoned cart" or "Past purchasers"), or hand-pick recipients. See your reach before you launch.
2. **Review the AI draft** — Nudgen drafts personalized emails in your brand voice. You stay in control: edit, preview, and test before anything goes live.
3. **Let the automation do the rest** — Launch a one-time send or a sequence. Nudgen tracks engagement and can stop future nudges automatically once a customer hits your chosen goal.

## What makes Nudgen feel different

Traditional campaign suites are often optimized for maximum flexibility. Nudgen is optimized for speed-to-value.

- You don't need to build complex workflow branches for useful automation.
- You start from a business goal like cart recovery, welcome, or win-backs.
- You review the draft and audience before launch instead of managing a maze of settings.
- Campaigns can stop themselves once customers have already acted.

This makes Nudgen a perfect fit for teams that want outcomes from email marketing without spending their week in campaign setups.
