Brave, Opera, and other Chromium-based browsers can install the extension directly from the Chrome Web Store.
</Note>

## Setup Instructions

1. **Visit the Store**: Click the link above for your browser.
2. **Add Extension**: Click "Add to Chrome" (or equivalent) and confirm the permissions.
3. **Pin to Toolbar**: For easy access, click the puzzle icon in your browser toolbar and pin **Lead Email Finder**.
4. **Start Finding**: Open any profile or website and click the extension icon to begin.

<Note>
You can use the extension for free with limited daily scans. For unlimited scanning, upgrade to a [Pro plan](https://nudgen.net/pricing).
</Note>
