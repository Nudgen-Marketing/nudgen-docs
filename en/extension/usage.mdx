## Workflow

1. **Navigate**: Visit a supported social profile (**LinkedIn, Facebook, X/Twitter**) or corporate website.
2. **Scan Page**: Click the extension icon and select **Scan Page** to identify potential leads on the current page.
3. **Validate All**: Click **Validate All** to perform real-time check against SMTP and MX records for all found emails.
4. **Export**: Click **Export CSV** to download your results for use in any CRM.

## Exporting Leads

After finding emails, you can export them for use in your marketing campaigns or other tools.

### Verification States

- <Icon icon="circle-check" color="green" /> **Verified**: High confidence, safe to send.
- <Icon icon="circle-info" color="orange" /> **Inconclusive**: We found a pattern, but the server didn't confirm (catch-all).
- <Icon icon="circle-xmark" color="red" /> **Invalid**: No valid email found or mailbox is full.

<Tip>
To ensure the best deliverability, prioritize leads with the **Verified** status.
</Tip>
