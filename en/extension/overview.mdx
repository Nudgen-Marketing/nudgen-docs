The **Lead Email Finder** is a free, high-accuracy browser extension designed to help you discover professional email addresses instantly while browsing **LinkedIn, Facebook, X/Twitter**, and other professional networks. Trusted by **1000+ professionals**, it integrates seamlessly with your Nudgen account to verify and save leads effortlessly.

## Key Features

- **99.9% Verification Accuracy**: Our multi-step verification process ensures the emails you find are valid and safe to send, eliminating bounces.
- **Extract Leads Anywhere**: Scan profiles on **LinkedIn, Facebook, X/Twitter**, or any company website to find professional email addresses in seconds.
- **Real-time Verification**: Built-in SMTP and MX record verification checks every email instantly to ensure it actually exists.
- **Export Anywhere**: Download your found leads as a clean CSV format for any CRM or database.

## How it works

1. **Browse**: Open any social profile (**LinkedIn, Facebook, X**) or corporate website.
2. **Scan**: The extension automatically identifies potential leads on the page.
3. **Verify**: The tool verifies found emails in real-time against server records.
4. **Export**: Export your leads instantly as a CSV file.
