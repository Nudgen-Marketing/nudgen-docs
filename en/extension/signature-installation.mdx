After creating your signature in the [Editor](/en/extension/signature-usage), you need to install it in your email client to start using it.

## Most Common Clients

> Follow [this](https://pdf.wondershare.com/signature/insert-html-signature-in-gmail.html) to add signature to Gmail. Check [this](https://www.christopherbolt.com/support/knowledgebase/24/Installing-HTML-email-signatures-in-Microsoft-Outlook.html) out for installing HTML signature in Outlook.

## General Installation Steps

If you are using a different email client, follow these general steps:

1.  In the Signature Generator preview, click **Copy to Clipboard**.
2.  Open your email client's **Settings** or **Preferences**.
3.  Locate the **Signature** section (often under "Mail" or "Composing").
4.  If there is an existing signature, delete it or create a new one.
5.  Paste (`Ctrl + V` or `Cmd + V`) your copied signature into the editor.
6.  **Save** your settings.

<Tip>
  If your signature looks slightly different in certain clients, ensure that your client is configured to send emails in **HTML/Rich Text** format rather than Plain Text.
</Tip>
