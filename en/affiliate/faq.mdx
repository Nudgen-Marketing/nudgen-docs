## Frequently Asked Questions

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="How do I join the affiliate program?">
    Click the **Become a Partner** button on the affiliate page. You'll be redirected to your affiliate dashboard where you can sign up immediately. No manual approval needed.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="How does the Lifetime payout work?">
    It's true passive income. As long as your referred user maintains their paid subscription and doesn't request a refund, you'll receive your 10% commission every single month for the life of that customer.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="What payment methods do you support?">
    Currently, we only allow payouts via **PayPal**. Please ensure your PayPal email address is correctly configured in your partner dashboard to receive your monthly earnings.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="How long is the cookie duration?">
    We use a **30-day tracking cookie**. If someone clicks your link and signs up for a paid plan anytime within 30 days, they are permanently linked to your account.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Can I use paid ads to promote my link?">
    Yes! Unlike many programs, we allow you to run paid advertisements to drive traffic to your referral link. You have full freedom to market as you see fit.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Can I refer myself or my own businesses?">
    No, self-referrals are strictly prohibited and considered illegal within our program. Using your own affiliate link to create accounts for yourself or your own companies will result in disqualification and forfeiture of commissions.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Is there a minimum payout threshold?">
    Yes, the minimum payout threshold is **$50**. If your earnings are below this amount, they will automatically roll forward to the next month until the threshold is met.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
