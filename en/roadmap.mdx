Planned features, in priority order. Dates and scope may change as we build.

## 1. Campaign A/B testing

Run A/B tests on subject lines or email content. Send variants to segments of your audience and compare performance (opens, clicks, conversions). Use the results to pick a winner or roll out the best variant to the rest of your list.

## 2. Support more combined stop rules

We will add more conditions for stop rules and allow you to combine them. When the combined conditions are met, the campaign automatically pauses or stops, giving you finer control and better protection for deliverability and list health.

## 3. Behavioral tracking SDK and early churn prevention

A lightweight SDK you embed in your product to track individual customer behavior — page visits, feature usage, inactivity patterns, and engagement signals — at the contact level. Unlike traditional analytics that show you aggregate trends after the fact, Nudgen's SDK is built for action: it detects early churn signals per contact, scores churn risk in real time, and automatically suggests re-engagement campaigns to warm contacts back up before they leave. Stop watching customers walk away — intervene while you still can.
