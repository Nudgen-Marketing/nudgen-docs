Use this checklist to get your workspace and first campaign in place. Each step links to the relevant guide.

## 1. Sign in and confirm workspace

- [ ] Go to [nudgen.net](https://nudgen.net) and sign in with Google.
- [ ] You land on the **Dashboard**. Confirm the workspace (team) in the sidebar header matches the one you want. Switch with the team switcher if you have multiple workspaces.

<Card title="Workspaces and roles" icon="users" href="/en/concepts/workspaces-and-roles">
  How teams, members, and permissions work.
</Card>

## 2. Invite your team (optional)

- [ ] Go to **Settings** → **Team**.
- [ ] Invite members by email. Copy the invite link or resend as needed.
- [ ] Assign roles and remove or change access as needed.

<Card title="Team settings" icon="user-group" href="/en/settings/team">
  Roles, invitations, and member management.
</Card>

## 3. Upload contacts

- [ ] Open **Contacts** and upload a CSV (email required, name optional) or add contacts manually.
- [ ] Add tags if you use segmentation (e.g. `trial`, `churned`).
- [ ] Confirm contacts appear and are within your plan limits.

<Card title="Contacts overview" icon="users" href="/en/contacts/overview">
  Encryption, masking, and segmentation.
</Card>
<Card title="CSV upload" icon="file-csv" href="/en/contacts/upload">
  Format, validation, and size limits.
</Card>

## 4. Configure brand and signatures

- [ ] Go to **Brand** and create or select a brand config. Choose a tone preset and optionally add a custom prompt or example emails.
- [ ] Go to **Settings** → **Signatures** and create at least one signature. You'll attach it when creating campaigns.
- [ ] Optionally set **Settings** → **Brand** (brand identity: name, reply-to email, logo) for From headers and AI context.

<Card title="Brand overview" icon="palette" href="/en/brand/overview">
  Tone presets and custom prompts.
</Card>
<Card title="Signatures" icon="pen-nib" href="/en/settings/signatures">
  Create and attach signatures to campaigns.
</Card>

## 5. Send your first campaign

- [ ] Go to **Campaigns** → **New campaign**.
- [ ] Complete **Campaign Setup** (type, audience, CTA link, brand, signature, goal).
- [ ] In **Content Generation**, generate copy, edit if needed, preview, and send a test email.
- [ ] In **Finalize**, set stop rules for drip if needed, then **Send now** or **Schedule**.

<Card title="Quickstart" icon="rocket" href="/en/quickstart">
  Step-by-step in about 5 minutes.
</Card>
<Card title="Create campaigns" icon="paper-plane" href="/en/campaigns/create">
  Full wizard and options.
</Card>

## 6. Review analytics

- [ ] Open the **Dashboard** to see overview metrics and the campaigns table.
- [ ] Open a campaign detail page for recipient-level stats and time-series.
