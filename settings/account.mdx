## What this page helps you do

Find and manage your account-level settings. This page points you to the right place for profile, team, signatures, and brand configuration.

## Before you start

- A Nudgen account
- Owner or admin role (some settings may be restricted)

## Settings overview

Go to **Settings** in the dashboard to access:

| Section       | What you can do                                      | Doc           |
| ------------- | ---------------------------------------------------- | ------------- |
| **Team**      | Invite members, assign roles, manage permissions     | [Team settings](/settings/team) |
| **Brand**     | Brand identity: name, description, reply-to email, logo | [Brand overview](/brand/overview) (see also **Brand** in sidebar for tone configs) |
| **Signatures** | Create and edit email signatures for campaigns     | [Signatures](/settings/signatures) |

## Workspace and account

Your account can belong to **multiple workspaces** (teams). The **active workspace** is shown in the sidebar header; use the team switcher to change it. All data (contacts, campaigns, brand configs, billing) is scoped to the active workspace. Your profile (name, email, avatar) is account-level and used for test emails, team member display, and activity.

## What happens next

- [Manage your team](/settings/team) — invite collaborators and set roles.
- [Set up signatures](/settings/signatures) — add signatures to campaign emails.
- [Configure brand tone](/brand/overview) — control how AI generates content.
