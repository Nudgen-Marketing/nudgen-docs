## What this page helps you do

Set the voice and style of AI-generated emails so they sound like your brand, not a generic template. You can use presets, add custom instructions, and provide example emails for calibration.

## Before you start

- A Nudgen account
- A clear idea of how you want to sound (e.g. casual, professional, urgent)

## Preset tones

Choose one of four presets to shape the AI's default style:

| Preset        | Best for                              | Example vibe                         |
| ------------- | ------------------------------------- | ------------------------------------ |
| **Friendly**  | E-commerce, shops, lifestyle products     | Warm, approachable, conversational    |
| **Professional** | B2B, finance, formal contexts     | Clear, polished, respectful           |
| **Playful**   | Consumer apps, Duolingo-style nudges   | Fun, light humor, friendly urgency   |
| **Urgent**    | Time-sensitive offers, winbacks       | Direct, action-focused, scarcity      |

You can change the preset anytime. New campaigns use the active preset unless you pick a different brand config.

## Custom prompt

Add extra instructions so the AI matches your voice more closely. Examples:

- "Use short sentences. Avoid jargon."
- "Reference the user's name once in the first paragraph."
- "End with a single clear CTA. No multiple links."
- "Tone should feel like a helpful colleague, not salesy."

These are combined with the preset and campaign goal when generating content.

## Example emails

Provide 1–3 example emails that reflect the tone you want. The AI uses them as references when generating new content. Include:

- Subject lines you like
- Opening and closing style
- Level of formality
- CTA style

<Note>
Examples are for tone calibration only. The AI does not copy your text; it learns the style and applies it to new campaigns.
</Note>

## Multiple brand configs

You can save several brand configs (e.g. "Product Launch", "Re-engagement", "Support"). When creating a campaign, select which config to use. This lets you switch tone by campaign type without changing a single global setting.

## What happens next

- [Create a campaign](/en/campaigns/create) and select your brand config. AI will use your tone and prompts.
- Regenerate content if the output doesn't match — you can tweak the prompt and try again.

## Troubleshooting

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Generated emails don't match my examples">
    Add more specific instructions in the custom prompt. Ensure your examples clearly show the tone you want (formal vs casual, short vs long).
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Can I use different tones for different campaigns?">
    Yes. Create multiple brand configs and choose one per campaign.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
