---

Planned features, in priority order. Dates and scope may change as we build.

## 1. Email templates

Reusable email templates so you can save layouts and copy and reuse them across campaigns. Create a template once, then select it when creating a campaign and customize as needed. We also support **dynamic variables** using Jinja templates—e.g. contact name, product name, and other per-recipient or per-campaign data—so each send can be personalized. Reduces repetition and keeps branding consistent.

## 2. Export CSV/Excel report

Export campaign and contact data as CSV or Excel. Download overview metrics, recipient-level activity, or contact lists for reporting, backup, or use in other tools.

## 3. Campaign A/B testing

Run A/B tests on subject lines or email content. Send variants to segments of your audience and compare performance (opens, clicks, conversions). Use the results to pick a winner or roll out the best variant to the rest of your list.

## 4. Mobile version support

A mobile-optimized experience so you can check the dashboard, review campaigns, and take key actions from phones and tablets. Responsive layouts and touch-friendly controls for on-the-go use.

## 5. CRM API integration

Integrate Nudgen with your CRM via API: sync contacts, trigger campaigns from CRM events, or push campaign results back. Enables programmatic workflows and keeps contact data in sync across systems.

## 6. MCP support

**MCP (Model Context Protocol) support** so LLMs—such as OpenClaw and other MCP-enabled assistants—can connect to Nudgen and control campaigns. Create, launch, pause, or monitor campaigns from your preferred AI environment with a consistent, tool-based interface.

## 7. Support more combined stop rules

We will add more conditions for stop rules and allow you to combine them. When the combined conditions are met, the campaign automatically pauses or stops, giving you finer control and better protection for deliverability and list health.

## 8. AI recommendation for campaign based on data insight

Recommendations driven by your campaign and contact data: suggested send times, subject lines, or segments based on past performance and engagement. Use data-backed suggestions to improve opens, clicks, and conversions without guesswork.

## 9. Multi-language support (i18n)

Full internationalization support across the entire Nudgen ecosystem. We are bringing multiple language options to the landing page, documentation site, and the main application dashboard to provide a seamless experience for our global user base.
