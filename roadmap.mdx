Planned features, in priority order. Dates and scope may change as we build.

## 1. Email templates

Reusable email templates so you can save layouts and copy and reuse them across campaigns. Create a template once, then select it when creating a campaign and customize as needed. Reduces repetition and keeps branding consistent.

## 2. Campaign A/B testing

Run A/B tests on subject lines or email content. Send variants to segments of your audience and compare performance (opens, clicks, conversions). Use the results to pick a winner or roll out the best variant to the rest of your list.

## 3. Export CSV/Excel report

Export campaign and contact data as CSV or Excel. Download overview metrics, recipient-level activity, or contact lists for reporting, backup, or use in other tools.

## 4. Mobile version support

A mobile-optimized experience so you can check the dashboard, review campaigns, and take key actions from phones and tablets. Responsive layouts and touch-friendly controls for on-the-go use.

## 5. CRM API integration

Integrate Nudgen with your CRM via API: sync contacts, trigger campaigns from CRM events, or push campaign results back. Enables programmatic workflows and keeps contact data in sync across systems.
